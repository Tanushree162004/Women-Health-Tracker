Understanding Macros and Nutrients

Macronutrients are the nutrients that provide calories or energy. They are:

  • Proteins: Essential for building and repairing tissues.
  • Carbohydrates: The body's main source of energy.
  • Fats: Important for energy and cell function.

Micronutrients are vitamins and minerals required in smaller amounts, but are crucial for proper body function.
